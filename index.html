About Dr. Karim Barsoum

Dr. Karim Barsoum is a Coptic Egyptian-American living and studying in Southern California. He comes from a family of world-renowned dentists, spanning 3 generations and counting. Karim will soon start practicing at Barsoum Dental in Upland and Chino, under the mentorship and guidance of his father, Dr. Maher Barsoum.

Between all the intense studying and practicing of dental school, Karim enjoys long distance biking and plane-spotting, especially at LAX with an In-N-Out burger in hand.
